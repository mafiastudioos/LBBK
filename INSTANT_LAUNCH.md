## 🌐 **METHOD 1: Vercel Drag & Drop (2 minutes)**

### **Step 1: Go to Vercel**
1. Open your browser
2. Go to: **[vercel.com](https://vercel.com)**
3. Click **"Start Deploying"** or **"Sign Up"**
4. Sign up with GitHub, Google, or email (free)

### **Step 2: Deploy**
1. You'll see a **drag & drop area**
2. **Drag your `build` folder** into the deploy area
3. **OR** click "Browse" and select your `build` folder
4. Click **"Deploy"**
5. **Wait 30 seconds** - Your site goes live! 🎉

### **Step 3: Get Your URL**
You'll get a URL like:
```
https://lashbeautybykim-abc123.vercel.app
```
